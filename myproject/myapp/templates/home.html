How to Apply for a New/Replacement ID

  1. Step 1: Visit the PUP ID Application Portal and click on the "Apply Now" button.
  2. Step 2: Select the reason for your application (e.g., Transferee, Shiftee, Late Filing, Damaged, Old ID, Correction of Entry).
  3. Step 3: Select your academic information (e.g., LHS, SHS, Undergrad, Graduate School, Open University, College of Law, Institute of Technology).
  4. Step 4: Fill out the application form with your personal details, including:
    • First Name
    • Middle Name
    • Last Name
    • Student Number (format: xxxx-xxxxx-MN-x)
    • Gender
    • Date of Birth
    • College
    • Course, Year, & Section
    • Home Address
    • Phone/Mobile Number (format: +63xxxxxxxxxx)
    • Email Address (format: example@email.com)
  5. Step 5: Provide emergency contact details, including:
    • Complete Name
    • Phone/Mobile Number
    • Address
  6. Step 6: Review your application and ensure all details are correct.
  7. Step 7: Click the "Submit" button to complete your application.
  8. Step 8: Wait for the confirmation email with further instructions on the ID pick-up process.
